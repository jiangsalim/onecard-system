## PART 4: DATABASE DECISION (5 min)

### Option A: STANDALONE (Recommended)
*"OneCard becomes your complete system"*

| What | How |
|------|-----|
| Students | One-time Excel upload |
| Payments | Weekly Excel upload (MTN/Airtel reports) |
| Database | Cloud hosted |
| Internet | Only for uploads |
| Cost | ~$14/month (~50,000 UGX) |

**Best if:** You want a fresh start.

---

### Option B: CONNECTED
*"OneCard reads from your existing database"*

| What | How |
|------|-----|
| Students | Live from your database |
| Payments | Live from your database |
| Database | Your server (must be online 24/7) |
| Internet | Always required |
| Cost | ~$14/month + IT setup |

**Best if:** You want to keep current system.

---

### Comparison:

| Feature | Option A | Option B |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Setup time | 1 week | 2-3 weeks |
| Monthly cost | ~$14 | ~$14 |
| Internet | Uploads only | 24/7 |
| IT support | Minimal | Moderate |
